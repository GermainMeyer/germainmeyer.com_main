Intuitive Teleoperation Device for Neuroendovascular Surgery

REHAssist - EPFL

September-January 2022

Removing blood clots requires high dexterity, while the X-rays needed to see the procedure are harmful for the surgeon. Force feedback teleoperation is a solution that does not compromise dexterity or safety.

Approach

  • Two scroll wheels for catheter translation and rotation control with force feedback improve the surgeon's understanding and control of their actions.

Design exploration

  • Functional decomposition.
  • Morphological matrix.
  • Decision-making matrix.

Mechanical sizing and design

  • Load calculation.
  • Desired force feedback.
  • Required precision.

Results

  • Fabrication and assembly.
  • The device reaches a precision of 100 um.
  • The device provides force feedback up to 10 N.
